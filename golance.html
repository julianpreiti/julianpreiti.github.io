  • Tools: Figma, Google Meet

Project Overview

GoLance is a freelance platform that connects companies and individuals with qualified freelancers from various fields. From short to long-term projects, goLance provides a platform for efficient and effective collaboration between clients and freelancers.

As a UX/UI designer in the GoLance team, my mission was to design wireframes for the applicant's screens and job postings. I started by analyzing the competition to identify design patterns and then interviewed potential users to finish creating the wireframes.

Identifying Competitors Design Patterns

Before designing the screens, I researched design strategies on other freelance platforms to identify effective design patterns, giving me valuable insights into structuring applicant information and enabling efficient implementation within the project timeline.

Research

My first step was collecting valuable information about users who use freelance platforms. To do this, I conducted semi-structured interviews to validate our user archetypes, understand their needs, and gain insights that could help improve our product.

What Do The Users Think?

Wireframes

Before designing the screens, I researched design patterns in other DeFi apps. Given the short timeline of the project, this allowed me to quickly gain insight into effective design strategies and incorporate them into the solution.

Postings screen

The purpose of this wireframe is to show the published posts, users can manage them, pause, delete, edit, and view post statistics.

Applicants Screen

The purpose of this wireframe is to display the list of project applicants, allowing users to compare, manage, filter, and connect with them. Users can view detailed applicant profiles and filter applicants based on various criteria.
