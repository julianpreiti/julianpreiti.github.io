Project Overview

Bee Well Inc. is a B2C eCommerce site that sought to rebrand and shift their target demographic within the burgeoning beauty/wellness space. As a small brick and mortar shop, their main problem was appealing to a younger online audience on a national scale.

I was tasked with designing a new brand identity for the company's online launch. Using the client's guidelines, my goal was to produce visually attractive and easily usable designs for their new desktop platform.

Working from scratch allowed me to create a brand new style tile and reform their outdated user model while remaining true to the company's homegrown roots.
