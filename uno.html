UNO

  • Year: 2022
  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Google Meet
  • Deliverables: High fidelity screens, UI Kit, Research

Project Overview

UNO is a DeFi super app that enables you to manage your digital assets all in one place. With UNO, you can buy, swap, and send tokens instantly, while keeping your wallet and funds fully under your control.

As a UX/UI designer on the UNO team, I focused on designing the onboarding process to create a seamless and intuitive user experience. By analyzing the competition and identifying areas for improvement, I developed a user-friendly onboarding flow that guides users through the app's features and functions.

Identifying Competitors Design Patterns

Before designing the screens, I researched design patterns in other DeFi apps. Given the short timeline of the project, this allowed me to quickly gain insight into effective design strategies and incorporate them into the solution.

The Importance of Onboarding Design Elements

Exploring UNO's Features

Presenting the App's Features

Users can explore all of the app's features through a presentation that provides an overview of its features. By exploring the presentation, users can better understand what the application offers. If users are already familiar with the app and don't need the presentation, they can skip it and go directly to the login page.

Take Control of Your Journey

Facilitate Your Progress Tracking with The Progress Bar

The Progress Bar in the app is a powerful element that keeps users informed about their progress. It shows how far they've come and how much further they need to go, allowing them to keep track of their journey through the app.

Secure Your UNO Account with Extra Protection

Improve Account Safety with Two-Step Authentication

Users have the option to add a two-step authenticator to enhance their account security. While this step is optional for those who do not want to add verification at that time, enabling two-step authentication can significantly improve the safety of the account.

Effortlessly Restore Access to Your Account.

Forgot Your Password? It Happens to Everyone

Forgetting your password happens to everyone. Users can easily reset it using the email address associated with their account.

Simple Registration and Login process

The Importance of Login and Sign Up

The registration and login processes are crucial steps to access the application's functions. To ensure a smooth user experience, users have a fast registration and login process.
